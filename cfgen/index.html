CFGen

A Team Fortress 2 custom config generator

This tool will overwrite all of your binds. It is recommended to be installed on a blank TF2, without any prior configs installed. Please make a backup of your CFG and custom folders before using this if you want to save your old binds. You can wipe your configs by clearing your custom and cfg folders and launching TF2 with -autoconfig launch option.
This tool is intended to be used with QWERTY keyboards, anything else is unsupported.

Comanglia's "Toaster" config

Bare minimum, with a focus on simply running the game.

This is probably the ugliest looking config you can get here. It disables a lot of things, and gives your TF2 that nice "blocky" oldschool look.

Source

This config can actually underperform on moderate hardware, even achieving lower frames than Comanglia's Stability config on identical hardware.

Pros

  • Maximum FPS for underpowered computers
  • Minimizes visual clutter
  • Oldschool™ feeling

Cons

  • Minor FPS gain for powerful PCs
  • Sharp, distracting blocky textures
  • Disables glow outline on team mates/mission objective/Scottish Resistance stickies
  • No sheens on killstreak weapons
  • No shadows and most props are disabled
  • No 3D skybox

Comanglia's "Stability" config

A solid option for older PCs, with a focus on stability.

Source

Pros

  • Decent fps gain for underpowered computers
  • A great choice for midrange PCs
  • Minimizes visual clutter
  • Smoother textures compared to Comanglia's Toaster config

Cons

  • Small fps gain for decent PCs
  • Disables glow outline on team mates/mission objective/Scottish Resistance stickies
  • No sheens on killstreak weapons
  • No shadows and some props are disabled
  • No 3D skybox

Comanglia's "Max FPS for High Quality PCs" config

For modern multi-core PCs, with a focus on maximum FPS while retaining modern graphics settings. Looks good, performs well.

Source

Requires quad-core CPU or better

Pros

  • Decent fps gain for good computers
  • A great performance choice for a modern/modern a few years ago PCs
  • Looks good, textures are bright and shine
  • Sheens on killstreak weapons
  • Enables glow outline on team mates/mission objective/Scottish Resistance stickies
  • Works with -dxlevel 81

Cons

  • Small/No FPS gain for old PCs
  • May be slower than Comanglia Stability config depending on your video card
  • Poor shadows and a few props are still disabled
  • Still not look as good as vanilla TF2
  • No 3D skybox

Comanglia's "Cinema" config

Take a look at this. This is TF2 at its maximum potential. This is what quality water and 3D skyboxes look like.

Source

You need a high spec PC to run this comfortably. Designed for rendering videos at high quality, not for realtime gameplay.

Pros

  • So nice and shiny
  • HDR resolution textures, everything looks as good as it was originally intended.
  • Even more bright sheens on killstreak weapons
  • Enables glow outline on team mates/mission objective/Scottish Resistance stickies
  • Does not remove anything, it's even a more enhanced than the highest graphical settings possible in the game's menu
  • Good stability with demo replays

Cons

  • The worst performance choice for any PC
  • Literally unplayable unless you have a high powered computer
